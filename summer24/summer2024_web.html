The summer program on "highly efficient numerical methods" 2024 will be held on July 15- August 16, 2024 on the Zhuhai campus of Beijing Normal University. This conference will bring together researchers of all career stages and graduate students, who work or are interested in numerical analysis and applications.

Registration deadline: July 1, 2024
